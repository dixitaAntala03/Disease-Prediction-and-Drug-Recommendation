#Diabetes 
Chronic condition affecting blood sugar levels due to insulin dysfunction, potentially leading to serious complications.

#Heart attack
Sudden blockage of blood flow to the heart, causing chest pain, shortness of breath, and potential death.

#Hypothyroidism
Thyroid gland underactive, causing fatigue, weight gain, cold sensitivity, and potential complications if untreated.

#Malaria
Mosquito-borne infectious disease causing fever, chills, flu-like symptoms, and potentially life-threatening complications if untreated.

#Gastroenteritis
Inflammation of the stomach and intestines, causing diarrhea, vomiting, abdominal pain, and dehydration, often viral.

#Bronchial Asthma
Chronic respiratory condition causing airway inflammation, constriction, coughing, and wheezing, triggered by various factors, often allergens.

#Migraine
Neurological disorder causing severe headaches, often accompanied by nausea, sensitivity to light and sound, and visual disturbances.

#Cervical spondylosis
Degenerative condition affecting the cervical spine, causing neck pain, stiffness, and potentially nerve compression symptoms like numbness.

#Dengue
Mosquito-borne viral infection causing high fever, severe joint and muscle pain, and potentially life-threatening complications.

#Common Cold
Mild viral infection of the upper respiratory tract, causing coughing, sneezing, congestion, and sore throat.
